🎓 What is CampusCoin?
CampusCoin is a university-exclusive, peer-to-peer task marketplace and social community. It is designed to help students monetize their skills and get help with day-to-day needs—ranging from debugging code and tutoring to running campus errands.

Instead of handling sensitive financial transactions directly, the platform uses a hybrid system: real money is transferred safely between students using standard UPI apps (like PhonePe or GPay) 📱💸, while a virtual utility token called CampusCoins is used to manage platform trust, prevent spam, and reward positive community engagement 🛡️🪙.

---

🔄 How It Works: The Task & Payment Loop
📝 Posting a Task: A student needs help (e.g., "Fix my React bug for ₹100"). They post this task to the marketplace.

🤝 Expressing Interest: Another student sees the task and clicks the "100% Interested" button. This immediately opens a secure, private chat room between the two peers.

💬 Negotiation: Inside the chat, the two students discuss the requirements and agree on a final price.

💳 Payment via UPI: Once the work is done, the person who posted the task clicks a "Pay" button inside the chat. This generates a deep-link that automatically opens their preferred UPI app on their phone, pre-filled with the exact amount and the helper's details. The payment is settled safely outside the app, but a receipt is automatically logged in the chat.

🪙 How It Works: The CampusCoin Economy
CampusCoins are not a 1:1 replacement for real money. They are "trust tokens" that keep the platform ecosystem balanced and running smoothly.

---

📉 How Students Spend Coins (The Sink):

📌 Listing Fees: It costs a small amount of coins to post a task. This prevents users from spamming the feed with fake or low-effort requests.

🎯 Application Fees: It costs coins to click "100% Interested," stopping people from blindly applying to every single job on the board.

🚀 Visibility Boosts: Students can spend a larger chunk of coins to "Boost" their task, pinning it to the top of the feed with a neon highlight so it gets noticed and completed faster.

---

📈 How Students Earn Coins (The Faucet):

🏆 Good Citizen Bounties: When a student successfully completes a task and the poster approves it, the platform automatically rewards the helper with fresh coins. (Note: The reward for completing a task is mathematically lower than the fee to post one, making it impossible for friends to "farm" fake tasks for infinite coins).

🌟 Community Karma: Students earn coins when their posts on the "Flex" community feed (like sharing study notes or offering a skill) receive likes from peers.

✅ Milestones & Streaks: Students earn coins for verifying their university email, completing their profile, or simply logging into the app daily.
